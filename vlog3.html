    Unigold Foods Ltd-University of Benin Poultry Promotional Video

  • Ada Eko
  • 2022

In a groundbreaking collaboration, Unigold Foods Ltd has partnered with the University of Benin (UNIBEN) Poultry to launch a promotional video that highlights their joint efforts in promoting sustainable poultry farming practices and enhancing food security. This initiative showcases the synergy between a leading food production company and an academic powerhouse to set a new standard in poultry production.

Feature Highlights The video captures: State-of-the-Art Facilities: A glimpse into the advanced poultry infrastructure at UNIBEN Farms, designed to ensure optimal conditions for poultry health and productivity. Sustainability in Action: Demonstrating environmentally friendly practices, including waste management and efficient feeding systems. Quality Assurance: Highlighting Unigold Foods’ role in ensuring that every poultry product meets international quality and safety standards. Empowering Students and Farmers: Showcasing the educational and training programs that equip students and local farmers with essential poultry farming skills.

Impact of the Partnership The partnership between Unigold Foods Ltd and UNIBEN Poultry aims to: Drive local food production to reduce reliance on imports. Promote youth involvement in agriculture through training and internships. Enhance Nigeria’s agricultural value chain with innovative approaches. Unigold Foods’ CEO remarked, "This promotional video is not just a showcase of our partnership but a testament to what can be achieved when industry and academia unite for a shared vision."
