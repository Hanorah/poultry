PARTNERS

Products

All over Nigeria, we work every day to bring quality wholesome food products to millions of Nigerians. Our belief is simple – put smiles on the faces of Nigerians.

As a modern multi-national food company, through our contact farming scheme, we produce a wide variety of products and cash crops, including animal protein products such as chicken, beef, and eggs. All our products are produced and sold under our brand, NARA™.

NARA FROZEN CHICKEN (OROBO)

Our NARA Frozen Chicken (Orobo) is expertly prepared, preserving the natural flavors of chicken while offering superior quality. It’s a preferred choice for many families and chefs, available across various retail channels.

NARA TOMATO PASTE

Our NARA Tomato Paste is made from the finest tomatoes, providing a rich, authentic flavor that enhances every dish. It is an essential ingredient in Nigerian kitchens, used in various culinary delights.

NARA EGGS

NARA Eggs are rich in nutrients and are sourced from healthy, well-fed chickens. They are available in various sizes and are perfect for daily use in homes and restaurants alike.

NARA PALM OIL

Our NARA Palm Oil is sourced from sustainable plantations and is refined to meet the highest quality standards. It is an essential product in many Nigerian dishes, giving a rich and authentic taste.

NARA POULTRY FEED

NARA Poultry Feed is designed to provide optimal nutrition for chickens, ensuring healthy growth and productivity. Our feed is available in different variants to meet the needs of various poultry breeds.

Animal Protein Products

Unigold Foods is also a leading provider of animal protein products such as beef, chicken, and eggs. Our products are readily available through major retail channels, grocery stores, and open markets across Nigeria.
