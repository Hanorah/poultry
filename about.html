Our Business and Operations

Poultry and Eggs Production Unigold Foods’ poultry division contributes significantly to the company, accounting for 75% of the food processing business. Our operations span the entire poultry supply chain, from upstream to downstream activities. The key products in our poultry business include: Day-old chicks Frozen packaged chicken (marketed under the Nara brand) Table eggs (marketed under the Nara brand) We take pride in the quality of our products. Our layers are raised from birds hatched within our own facilities, ensuring the highest quality eggs under the Nara brand. We currently have an installed capacity of producing 1,000 crates of eggs daily, sourced from both our domestic production and contract farmers. Unigold Foods operates a modern hatchery system, which is used to produce healthy day-old chicks, ensuring quality incubation for raising broilers and layers. We use high-quality natural feed produced at our own feed mill, ensuring our chickens are raised in the best possible conditions. Approximately 75% of our chickens are grown by our contract farmers. To meet growing demand, Unigold Foods is working to increase its processing capacity to 5,000 birds per hour by 2030. Our vision is to become the largest chicken processing and packaging company in Nigeria within the next 10 years, with an ambitious target of increasing broiler production by 20% annually.

Independent Local Producers

At Unigold Foods, we are committed to sourcing high-quality broilers and eggs from independent local producers. Our partnerships with local family farmers ensure the highest standards of animal husbandry and environmental practices. These relationships not only benefit our operations but also contribute significantly to the economic success of rural communities across Nigeria.

By purchasing livestock from a variety of local suppliers, we support local economies, decrease our carbon footprint, and promote sustainability. Our chicken and beef production facilities are strategically located in areas where livestock is raised, allowing us to buy locally and support the economic viability of surrounding communities.

Tomato Paste - Nara

Unigold Foods has made significant strides in the tomato paste market with our Nara brand. Since its introduction in 2013, Nara tomato paste has been a staple in Nigerian kitchens, helping families make healthy, fresh stews and meals. All tomatoes used for our paste are sourced from cooperative farming in Kano and Benue States, ensuring freshness and quality.

Red Palm Oil - Nara

Unigold Foods is also involved in the production of high-quality red palm oil, sourced from cooperatives and farmers in Edo, Delta, Abia, and Imo States. Our partnerships with smallholder oil palm farmers create economic opportunities for low-income Nigerians, providing them with a sustainable livelihood.

Sustainability and Environmental Responsibility

At Unigold Foods, sustainability is a core principle. We are committed to responsible environmental, social, and economic practices that will benefit both current and future generations.
